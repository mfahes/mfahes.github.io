Bio

I am a second-year PhD student at Astra-vision, a joint group between Inria and valeo.ai. I'm currently working on scene understanding in adverse conditions, under the supervision of Raoul de Charette, Tuan-Hung Vu , Andrei Bursuc and Patrick Pérez. I received an MSc degree in Mathematics, Vision and Learning from ENS Paris-Saclay, an engineering degree from Mines Paris, and a mechanical engineering degree from Lebanese University.
